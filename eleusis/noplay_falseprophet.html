No Play - False Prophet

No Play and a Prophet

What do you do if there is a Prophet and another player declares No Play? There are four possibilities in this case.

The player is correct and the Prophet is correct

The Prophet deals the player a new hand of cards with four fewer cards. If the player had four cards or less, they go out and the hand is over. Scoring begins.

The player is correct and the Prophet is incorrect

In other words the prophet says they player’s No Play is wrong. The Dealer says the propet is incorrect meaning the player’s No Play is right.

  • The Dealer deals the player a new hand with four fewer cards as above or the player goes out if they have four or less cards.
  • The Prophet becomes a False Prophet, they receive a five-card penalty, and play resumes without the Prophet.

The player is incorrect and the Prophet is correct

  • The Prophet tells the player they are incorrect.
  • The Prophet takes a good card from their hand and plays it.
  • Then the Prophet deals them a four-card penalty.

The player is incorrect and the Prophet is incorrect

In other words the player really does have a play, but the Prophet says their No Play was correct.

  • The Dealer takes a correct card from the player and plays it.
  • Since the player helped overthrow the Prophet, there are no penalty cards for the player.
  • The Prophet becomes a False Prophet and receives the five-card penalty.
  • Play resumes without the Prophet.
